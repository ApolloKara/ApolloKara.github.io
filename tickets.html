Accessibility
Yes! Fiddle & Fern is committed to inclusivity. The venue has accessible parking, smooth pathways, and wheelchair-accessible facilities. If you require additional assistance or have specific needs, please reach out to our team in advance so we can help make your experience as enjoyable as possible.
Important Information
All tickets to Fiddle & Fern are digital and must be presented at the gate, either on your phone or printed. Tickets are non-refundable, but they can be transferred to another person. If the event is postponed, your ticket will remain valid for the new date. Children under 12 enter free with a ticket-holding adult. Make sure to purchase tickets only through our official website or trusted partners to avoid scams.
