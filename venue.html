The Venue

Nestled in the heart of the Yarra Valley, Fiddle & Fern Festival takes place in one of the most picturesque regions of Victoria. Known for its stunning vineyards, lush rolling hills, and breathtaking landscapes, the Yarra Valley offers the perfect backdrop for our celebration of folk music and culture.

Just an hour's drive from Melbourne, our venue is easily accessible yet feels like a serene escape from the bustle of the city. Whether you're arriving to enjoy the festival's performances, food, or workshops, you'll be immersed in nature’s beauty, with breathtaking views of the surrounding valley and hills.

The valley's rich heritage and vibrant community make it the ideal place to experience both traditional and contemporary folk music while connecting with like-minded festival-goers in an unforgettable setting. dicta.
