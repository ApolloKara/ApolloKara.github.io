About Fiddle & Fern
Fiddle & Fern is a folk music festival in the Yarra Valley, celebrating music, culture, and community.
Where is the festival?
The festival takes place in the scenic Yarra Valley, known for its stunning landscapes and rich musical heritage.
Get Tickets
Tickets are available online and at select local outlets. Early bird discounts apply!
Car Parking
On-site parking is available, with priority spots for carpoolers and electric vehicles.
Whose Playing
Fiddle and fern is featruing Nathan Evans and Vinny Marchi for more information regarding them look at the Lineup Page!
Visitor Information
The festival is accessible by car and public transport. Wear comfy shoes, bring sunscreen, and hydrate. Nearby accommodations are available.
Camping
Choose from general camping or glamping. All sites are close to the festival. Book early! Amenities include bathrooms, food vendors, and fire pits.
