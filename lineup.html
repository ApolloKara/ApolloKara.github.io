Lineup

Nathan Evans

A dynamic folk artist known for bringing sea shanties to a global audience, Nathan Evans blends traditional storytelling with modern energy. With his rich vocals and engaging stage presence, he’s sure to get the crowd singing along at Fiddle & Fern. Expect rousing choruses, foot-stomping rhythms, and a fresh take on classic folk traditions. Originally a postman from Scotland, his journey from posting TikTok videos to topping international music charts is the kind of folk legend that belongs in song itself. His performances are filled with rousing choruses, foot-stomping rhythms, and an electrifying connection with the crowd—transforming every show into a communal celebration of music.

Vinny Marchi

With a voice that blends nostalgia and modern folk energy, Vinny Marchi has captured the hearts of millions online. Known for his hauntingly beautiful rendition of Misty Mountains and other soul-stirring covers, Vinny brings a fresh, digital-age spirit to traditional sounds. A rising sensation across TikTok and Instagram, he seamlessly bridges classic folk storytelling with today’s social media-driven world. Get ready for an unforgettable performance at Fiddle & Fern, where Vinny’s rich vocals and passion for music will fill the Yarra Valley air.
